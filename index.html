Heidelberg, Germany, Septemper 12th.
Co-located with the 2016 ACM International Joint Conference on Pervasive and Ubiquitous Computing (UbiComp 2016)

About

International Workshop on Ubiquitous Transportation will be held in conjunction with the 2016 ACM International Joint Conference on Pervasive and Ubiquitous Computing (UbiComp 2016) This workshop aims to gather professionals in industry and academia from both transportation and ubiquitous computing research domains to discuss the latest scientific results and practical use cases. The main objective is to identify the opportunities and challenges and gather best methods and practices of applying ubiquitous computing technologies in the transportation domain.








Topics of interest

Authors are invited to submit papers that are related to one of the areas listed below:

From data to situation awareness

Data processing (data acquisition, x2x, data fusion, data analysis)
Sensing, Crowdsourcing, mobile computing
Context- and situation-awareness
Context modelling, reasoning, decision-making
Personalization


Methods and algorithms

Machine learning
Statistical analysis
Big Data, Spatial Data, Open Data


Real world applications and experiments

Transportation services (road/rail)
Intelligent transportation systems, Co-operative ITS
Green (eco-, sustainable, safe) driving
Semi-autonomous/Autonomous driving








Paper submission

All the submitted articles must present original research and experience, not published or submitted anywhere else. All submissions should follow SIGCHI Extended Abstract Format and not exceed 8 pages. The templates are available at https://github.com/sigchi/Documents-Formats. The papers submitted must present original, unpublished experiences and research.

Proceedings

All the accepted articles will be included in the ACM Digital Library and supplemental proceedings of the UbiComp 2016.
